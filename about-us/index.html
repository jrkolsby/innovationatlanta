About Us

We are Innovation Atlanta

We are a global innovation acceleration firm. We collaborate with leaders and change agents at the world’s top companies to identify and pursue new growth opportunities, build innovation pipelines, and create disruptive new products, services, and businesses.

Our innovation acceleration approach is rooted in the 25+ years of each of our founding members, board members, and professors at Georgia Tech. We have a deep understanding of the processes necessary to launch successful innovations – developed through rigorous fieldwork across a variety of industries and business types.

We believe developing innovation pipeline is essential for long-term and sustainable growth that serves multiple stakeholders – employees, consumers, shareholders, and society at large.

Who should hire us?

Change Agents because you have the passion

Marketers because you create demand

Product Managers because you own innovation

Senior Executives because you’re responsible for growth

Why should you hire us?

1. We focus on growth through innovation which enables us to provide an end-to-end service based on proven techniques.

2. We’ve successfully solved a problem like yours before because we’re experienced in corporate settings, start-ups, and agencies.

3. We focus on tangible business results working with you as part of your team every step of the way.
