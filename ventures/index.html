Strategic Investment

MarketSquare Ventures is the strategic investment arm of Innovation Atlanta Innovation Acceleration. In evaluating which early stage products and companies to support, we leverage more than 20 years of applied research distilled into a set of proprietary tools and work practices that allow us to quickly screen opportunities and work side by side entrepreneurs to introduce and execute for our large innovation accleration clients.

Generally speaking, we ask five questions about any potential investment:

1. Is there an important problem that customers can’t address because existing solutions are too expensive?

2. Is there a disruptive way to solve the problem by being simpler, more convenient?

3. Can we design an economically attractive, scalable business model?

4. Does the team have the know-how to operate in an agile environment?

5. Do we have existing clients that can immediately benefit?
